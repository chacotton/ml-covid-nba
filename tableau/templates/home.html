Methodology

By leveraging state of the art machine learning, we were able to successfully analyze and predict a player's healthiness on a given day. Healthiness is relative scale of how likely a player is to peform at their standard level. This healthiness score tells us what level of output can be expected for any given game and allows us to maximize our predictive ability in games.

By adding in this healthiness score we were able to effectively predict the outcomes of games and from there we analyzed the impact of each player's health. Our primary goal was to measure how impactful a player's health was on a given game. To calculate impact we divided players into two categories. Active and Inactive An active players impact was measured as how their team's win probability changed based on how their individual health changed from the previous game. Inactive players had their health measured by considering how they would contribute if they were available. This impact measure is not meant to show how important a player is (although they tend to be correlated, rather it shows how their health status is impacting their team.
