Methodology

We leveraged state-of-the-art machine learning to accurately predict NBA games using our proprietary Health Score. This Health Score allows for maximization of correct win probabilities and generating player impact.

Insights

Using player impact we show the contribution of individual players to their team's overall performance. These contributions are broken down into single games, a team's season, and league-wide. On the league level we focus directly on Covid.

Impact is a team's change in win probability based on how healthy a player was yesterday vs today.

Game Planning

We allows users to see how a team's chance of to win changes based on which players are active for that game.

The NBA, like many corporations and businesses, has been largely affected by COVID-19. However, unlike many standard businesses that can track the effect of the pandemic on a spreadsheet or statistic, we have no way to tangibly mark the effects of COVID-19 across the NBA.
